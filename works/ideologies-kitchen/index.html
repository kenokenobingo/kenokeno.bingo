ideologies.kitchen

Ideolgies are fragmenting. Those fragments are published on the internet by their followers, using platforms embedded in their ideolgies: Most of the internet’s infrastructure is based on capitalist and libertarian thinking.
ideologies.kitchen produces merch for all of those fragmented ideologies, being in that sense agnostic. The actual goals of ideologies.kitchen are obviously of capitalist and libertarian nature: selling goods and providing an archive of the ideologies collected.
